Xena

Xena is a business run BY girls, FOR girls. We are dedicated to educating young girls about the importance of self love, positive body image, and mental and physical health. We provide practical tools in a chilled and relatable way to give these girls the best chance at living life to their fullest potential.

Our Mission

Our mission at Xena is to build girls who love who they are, believe they can do anything, and prioritise their health physically and mentally.

We recognise the harmful culture of dieting, overtraining and negative body image that the media creates.

We aim to provide science backed tools that can be implemented into young girls everyday lives to improve their overall health. We are the relatable, chill and positive influences young girls need!

Meet Sophie

Head Trainer Sophie is a Sydney based personal trainer with certs 3 and 4 in fitness. Sophie splits her time between her PT business, with clientelle all over the Eastern Suburbs and her studies to become a nutrition coach.

Bio: I have always had a passion for health and fitness from a young age, however I grew up in the times of social media and influencers. As a young person, social media is where I receive all of my information. Everything I learnt about being healthy, training, and nutrition came from social media. Anybody with a social media account can post advice, so there's no surprise that I went down an unhealthy track at such a young age. I copied whatever these influencers ate, drank, and did. Iâ€™m sure you don't need to guess how that went for me. My self confidence, body image, and overall self belief was severely impacted by this. I felt like I wasn't good enough, simply because I didn't look like these influencers on instagram.

I then decided to go and study, to educate myself on health and fitness. This led me on the path to create the XENA movement. I created this business as I believe girls need a positive influence, that can relate to them and their current lives. They need someone to tell them how to decipher from a positive influence, and a negative one. Someone to tell them that you don't need to look a certain way or fit into any sort of mould. Someone to tell them that who they are is exactly who they need to be.
